      Trending Questions

      Financial Aid Email
      Question: Hello! I received an email from accounting to remind me to pay tuition. But I also received an email from Financial Aid about technical issues. I'm a little confused on what to do.
      Answer: That is a great question! Please follow the steps on the Financial Aid email, it contains important information that you will need to follow. The Federal Financial Aid should be posted on your BYUI account on Wednesday, if you attended Winter 2022, and any remaining balance should go through E-refund in the following days. Please contact us if you have further questions.

      Advisor Appointment
      Question: How do I schedule an academic advising appointment?

      Answer: If you are a campus student or an online student in a major from 2017 or before, our contact center agents would be happy to set up an academic advising appointment for you! Please contact us by text, phone, or chat to schedule an appointment with an Academic Advisor. You can also set your up your own appointment by following the step by step walk through, see Advising - Schedule an Appointment. For online students in a major after 2017 please fill out the BYU-Pathway Advising Form.

      Ordering a BYU-Idaho Transcript
      Question: How do I order a BYU-Idaho transcript?
      Answer: To order a transcript, visit Order a BYU-Idaho Transcript and select the button that says "Order Official Transcript".
       

      Change Bank Account for FAFSA
      Question: How do I change the account my FAFSA money gets put into?
      Answer: FAFSA money usually goes to the bank account associated to your eRefund. If you want to learn how to change that bank account here's an article that can walk you through the steps: How Do I Enroll in eRefund?. Make you sure you scroll down to the part where it says "Changing Bank Account for eRefund". Hope this helps!!

      Transcript Evaluation Timeline
      Question: How long does it take for my transcripts to be evaluated?
      Answer: From the time that you request your transcripts to be sent to BYUIdaho, it generally takes 2-4 weeks for the admissions office to receive them. If all the necessary steps have been completed, and BYUIdaho has received your transcripts, it typically takes 2-4 weeks for them to be reviewed and processed. As soon as your transcripts are reviewed and processed, the transfer evaluation unit will send you a notification email to your student email address. More information about the steps that you need to complete as well as the time line, can be found at Guidelines.

      Forms

      Answer: You can find these services by going to the top of the OneStop portal and clicking on the black button that says "Services". You will find different categories, feel free to choose the service depending on what you need! Hope this helps!
