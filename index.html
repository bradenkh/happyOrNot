Trending Questions

Deferment Options
Question: How do I get a deferment?
Answer: For a list of deferment options available as well as the requirements for each, please refer to Deferment Options.

Dropping a class
Question: How do I drop a class?
Answer: You can refer to How to Drop/Withdraw from Class for a tutorial on how to drop a class. Depending on when you do that, there may be different penalties. You can find all of the important deadlines for dropping classes at Academic Deadlines. Please keep in mind that there is no tuition refund for classes that are dropped after the add/drop deadline.

Transcript Evaluation Timeline
Question: How long does it take for my transcripts to be evaluated?
Answer: From the time that you request your transcripts to be sent to BYU–Idaho, it generally takes 2-4 weeks for the admissions office to receive them. If all the necessary steps have been completed, and BYU–Idaho has received your transcripts, it typically takes 2-4 weeks for them to be reviewed and processed. As soon as your transcripts are reviewed and processed, the transfer evaluation unit will send you a notification email to your student email address. More information about the steps that you need to complete as well as the time line, can be found at Guidelines.

Tuition Refund
Question: How can I get a Tuition Refund?​​​​​​​
Answer: We invite you to visit Tuition Refund Policy to understand BYU-Idaho's tuition refund policy.​​​​​​​

Student Charges
Question: What if I have questions about specific charges on my BYU-I account?
Answer: You are able to view your student charge history on your BYU-Idaho account. Please refer to How to View Charges in Your Account for a walk-through on how to view your history.

If you still have questions after viewing your history...

  • University Store Charges: you can refer to How to View University Store Charges through the BYU-Idaho Website for a walk-through on how to view your University Store receipt.
  • All other charges: you can complete an Account Specific Information Request to receive clarification from Accounting Services about your student charges within one business day. 


Payments

Question: Is there a way that I can do monthly payments for my tuition? 
Answer: You can set up a Payment Plan to make monthly payments and prevent your debts from being sent to a Collections Agency (a Payment Plan will NOT prevent you from getting the additional 1% late fee every month). Complete the Payment Plan Request to set one up.
 

Tax form

Question: How do I get my tax forms?
Answer: If you are a student looking for your 1098-T that provides information about educational expenses that may qualify you for education-related tax credits, see How can I get my 1098-T Form?.
If you are an employee looking for your W-2 tax forms that are payroll related, see How can I view/print my W-2 online?
If you are a Canadian student looking for your TL11A (which is similar to the 1098-T form), see How can I request a TL11A (Canadian Tax Form)?


Advisor Appointment
Question: How do I schedule an academic advising appointment?
Answer: If you are a campus student or an online student in a major from 2017 or before, our contact center agents would be happy to set up an academic advising appointment for you! Please contact us by text, phone, or chat to schedule an appointment with an Academic Advisor.
You can also set your up your own appointment by following the step by step walk through, see Advising - Schedule an Appointment.
For online students in a major after 2017 please fill out the BYU-Pathway Advising Form
 

Edit personal information

Question: How do i change my Display name on my portal?
Answer: You can find the option to change your name under 'profile' on your old myBYU-I homepage. You can refer to Demographic Information Change for a step-by-step tutorial. If you are a BYU-Pathway Online student, you will need to contact BYU-Pathway Support for assistance. You can reach them by selecting the BYU-Pathway Support live chat option at the bottom right of the BYU-Pathway help page.
